About me

Hej! I'm Roemer (['rum…ôr]) and I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Clinical Epidemiology at the Leiden University Medical Center, the Netherlands. The main focus of my thesis is prediction modelling of patient-reported outcomes in kidney disease, although I also work on treatment trajectories, causal renal pharmacoepidemiology, and assessment in medical education. This highlights my three main academic interests: medicine, epidemiology, and education, which my background in medicine gives me ample opportunity to pursue. Besides indulging in these interests, you are likely to find me playing tabletop games, videogames, in a boulder hall, taking pictures, or talking too much about either the Lord of the Rings or trains.
